[[Gauss's Law]]: $\hspace{30px} \nabla \cdot \vec{E} = \frac{\rho}{\varepsilon_o}$

[[Gauss's Law (Magnetism)]]: $\hspace{30px} \nabla \cdot \vec{B} = 0$
